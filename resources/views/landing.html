Why to use this website?

This website is based on the recovery of items/individuals lost throughout the nation due to some reasons/factors.

How it works?

The solution that we plan is to develop a platform (Application) on which people can upload the details of the items that they lost at a particular place at a specified time. The person who finds can upload the pictures/details of the lost items that he finds from a particular place. The victim can then search on the platform by entering some details or location where he lost the item. All the items that match the description entered by the victim will then be displayed on the platform. If he finds anything that matches his details, he can then contact the person who uploaded the ad of finding any lost item.

What is scope of this website?

The application being developed is a platform to help people recover their lost items/individuals. The person who gets any of his items lost or a family member being lost can upload an ad on the platform providing all the necessary details sufficient enough to recover his belongings. He will give the expected time and location that he thinks is where he lost it. The finder is the person who finds any missing item and is willing to return it to its rightful owner. He can also upload an ad giving the details of the item and specifying the location and the time at which he found it. In case of finding a missing person, the finder will upload an ad but he will not keep the custody of the individual.
